## Clear Acrylic Square Rod

Clear acrylic square rod offers excellent optical clarity in a solid square profile — suited for displays, structural supports, and custom fabrication projects.

### Why Choose Square Rod?

- **Clean, modern profile** — the square cross-section suits contemporary designs
- **Excellent clarity** — transparent acrylic with superior light transmission
- **Solid construction** — strong and durable for structural and decorative use
- **Machinable** — easy to cut, drill, polish, and glue

### Extruded vs Cast

- **Extruded** — very good clarity, suited for indoor applications, machinable and glueable
- **Cast** — excellent clarity, UV stable, suited for external applications
