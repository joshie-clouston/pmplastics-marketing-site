## Fluorescent Edge Effect with Anti-Reflective Finish

Perspex Fluorescent Anti-Reflective combines the striking fluorescent edge-glow effect with a double-sided textured surface that reduces reflections. The soft sheer finish on both sides creates a translucent, diffused appearance while maintaining the vibrant "live-edge" effect that makes the Fluorescent range so distinctive.

This variant is designed for applications where glare reduction is important alongside bold colour impact.

Perspex Fluorescent Anti-Reflective is ideal for retail displays, point-of-sale installations, interior signage, window displays, and shelving systems where a bold fluorescent effect is needed without surface glare.
