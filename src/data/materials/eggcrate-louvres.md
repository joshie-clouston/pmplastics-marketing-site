## What Are Eggcrate Louvres?

Eggcrate louvres are interlocking grid panels used for light diffusion and air management. The distinctive cell pattern provides effective glare control in fluorescent light fittings and allows free air flow in ventilation and air conditioning applications.

## Key Properties

- **Light control:** Effective glare reduction and light diffusion
- **Air flow:** Open cell structure allows free air circulation
- **Weight:** Very lightweight
- **Installation:** Easy to cut and install with standard tools
- **Maintenance:** Low — wipe clean
- **Flame retardancy:** Flame-retardant grades available

## Available Options

### Cell Sizes
- 12.7 x 12.7 mm (1/2 inch)
- 15 x 15 mm

### Materials
- **Polystyrene:** Cost-effective, white finish
- **Aluminium:** Silver or white finish, higher durability

## Available Sizes

**Standard sheet sizes:** 1220 x 610 mm and 1220 x 1220 mm

**Depth options:** 12.7 mm (1/2 inch)

**Colours:** White, Silver (aluminium)

## Applications

Eggcrate louvres are widely used for fluorescent light diffusers, air conditioning return air grilles, ceiling panels, ventilation screens, shopfitting, and commercial fit-outs.
