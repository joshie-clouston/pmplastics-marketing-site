## Custom Plastic Design and Manufacturing

P&M Plastics design and manufacture a wide range of plastic products made from materials such as acrylic, Perspex, polycarbonate, PVC and aluminium composite. Our products are used across point-of-sale displays, illuminated signage, fabricated letters, logos, and air conditioning ductwork.

We also machine polyethylene and polypropylene into machine guards, signage, storage tanks, acid tanks, and components for food processing, electrical installations, engineering, trucks, and buses.

## Our Capabilities

With a full suite of CNC equipment and experienced fabricators on-site, we handle every stage of your project — from initial design through to finished product:

- **CNC Laser Cutting** — precision cuts in plastics, timber and metals
- **CNC Router Cutting** — intricate 2D and 3D machining across a wide range of materials
- **CNC Water Jet Cutting** — cuts virtually any material into any shape
- **CNC Vacuum Forming** — thermoforming over custom moulds for consistent production
- **Plastic Thermoforming** — shaping thermoplastics using specialised ovens
- **2D Laser Engraving & Etching** — detailed surface marking and branding
- **3D Laser Engraving** — textured, dimensional engraving for high-impact results

## Precision You Can Count On

The accuracy and consistency of our machined components guarantee we will always give our customers a first-class product. Whether it is a one-off prototype or a long production run, P&M Plastics delivers reliable results every time.
