## Eye-Catching Fluorescent Effects

Perspex Fluorescent is a specialist cast acrylic sheet featuring fluorescent edges that appear to glow under ambient light. The striking visual effect makes it a popular choice for architects, designers, and retailers seeking to create bold, attention-grabbing displays.

The material shares identical physical properties to standard Perspex cast acrylic, making it fully suitable for fabrication and thermoforming.

## Available Colours

- **4T56** — Fluorescent Mars Red
- **2T51** — Fluorescent Helios (Solar Yellow)
- **7T97** — Fluorescent Neptune Blue
- **6T66** — Fluorescent Acid Green
- **4T96** — Fluorescent Candy Pink
- **Lava Orange**

## Key Features

- **Intense, vibrant "live-edge" effect** that captures attention in any retail or design setting
- **All the benefits of Perspex acrylic** — excellent quality, performance, and design capability
- **Suitable for fabrication and thermoforming** with standard acrylic processing methods
- **Up to 17 times the impact resistance of glass**
- **100% recyclable**

## Typical Applications

Perspex Fluorescent is ideal for internal signage, retail point-of-sale displays, window displays, shelving systems, and interior design features where a bold, luminous edge effect is desired.
