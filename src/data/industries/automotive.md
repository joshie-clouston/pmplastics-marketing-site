## Automotive Plastic Products

P&M Plastics supplies a range of plastic products purpose-built for automotive and workshop environments — from vehicle flooring and matting to safety speed humps.

### Product Range

#### Small Stud Flooring
- **Dimensions:** 4.0 mm x 1 m x 10 m
- **Colour:** Black
- **Minimum cut length:** 1 metre
- Durable, anti-slip surface ideal for workshop and garage flooring

#### Speed Humps
- **Dimensions:** 1 m x 30 cm x 5 cm
- Straight ends for easy joining of multiple units
- Bolt-down installation for secure placement in car parks and driveways

#### Universal Tread Eze (U.T.E.) Matting
- **Dimensions:** 10 mm thick x 10 m roll
- Anti-slip surface with drain holes and lugs for superior grip and drainage
- **Colour:** Black
- Ideal for ute trays, trailers, and workshop floors
