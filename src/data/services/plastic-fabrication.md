## Gold Coast Plastic Fabrication Services

Plastic fabrication covers the most diversified range of processes performed on thermoplastic materials. At P&M Plastics, our services include cutting, machining, line bending, welding, gluing, diamond polishing, drilling, and assembly across a wide variety of thermoplastic materials.

With over 30 years of industry experience, we handle jobs of every scale — from [water tanks](/products/custom-liquid-transport-tanks) and large-format signage through to small retail fittings and one-off prototypes.

## Our CNC Capabilities

We operate modern CNC equipment to deliver precision results across every project:

- **[CNC Water Jet Cutting](/services/cnc-water-jet-cutting)** — cuts all types of plastics and other materials into virtually any shape, text, or design
- **[CNC Vacuum Forming](/services/cnc-vacuum-forming)** — multiple specialised machines with a variety of applications
- **[CNC Router Cutting](/services/cnc-router-cutting)** — versatile machines for cutting a wide selection of plastics and other materials
- **[CNC Laser Cutting](/services/cnc-laser-cutting)** — competitive pricing with superb turnaround for various materials
- **[Plastic Thermoforming](/services/plastic-thermo-forming)** — specialised ovens for multiple forming applications
- **[2D Laser Engraving](/services/2d-laser-engraving-etching) and [3D Laser Engraving](/services/3d-laser-engraving-etching)** — detailed engraving and etching with design assistance available
