## The World's Leading Acrylic Brand

Perspex is an internationally renowned brand of acrylic recognised for its quality and extensive product range. First developed in 1934 — originally used for Spitfire fighter plane canopies — Perspex has set the benchmark for cast acrylic performance for nearly a century.

P&M Plastics is an authorised Queensland reseller of the full Perspex range.

## Why Choose Perspex?

Perspex delivers exceptional weather performance and superior light transmission, making it the material of choice for architects, designers, and fabricators across Australia. Its extensive colour matching capability has made it the go-to acrylic for multinational brands requiring precise corporate imaging.

Clear acrylic sheets are backed by a **30-year warranty**, while coloured variants carry a **10-year warranty** — a testament to the durability and UV stability of the product.
