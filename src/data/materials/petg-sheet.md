## What Is PETG Sheet?

Clear PETG (Polyethylene Terephthalate Glycol) sheet is manufactured through extrusion, offering excellent clarity, high impact resistance, good transparency, and excellent thermoformability. It is a versatile material suited for demanding applications requiring durability and visual clarity.

## Key Properties

- **Light transmission:** 87–91%
- **Impact strength:** Excellent inherent toughness
- **Printability:** Outstanding
- **Rigidity:** Maintains high rigidity at elevated temperatures
- **Pre-drying:** Not required for processing
- **Dimensional stability:** Excellent
- **Water absorption:** Low
- **Specific gravity:** 1.27

**Note:** Not recommended for external use. UV-treated grades available on request.

## Available Sizes

**Standard sheet size:** 1220 x 2440 mm (8 x 4 ft) — limited oversized sheets available.

**Thickness options:** 0.5, 0.75, 1.0, 1.5, 2.0, 3.0 mm

**Colour:** Clear only

## Applications

PETG sheet is commonly used for screen printing, [signage](/industries/shopfitting-signage), point-of-sale displays, and industrial guards.
