## Sneeze Guard Installations Gallery

P&M Plastics manufactures custom sneeze guards in a range of materials and configurations to suit retail point of sale, hospitality, medical, and commercial environments. This gallery showcases installations across the Gold Coast and beyond.

### Types of Installations

- **Retail point-of-sale screens** — freestanding and counter-mounted guards for checkout counters, service desks, and reception areas
- **Food service guards** — protective barriers for bakeries, delis, buffets, and takeaway counters
- **Medical and pharmacy counters** — hygienic screening for pharmacies, pathology labs, and medical reception desks
- **Office front desks** — clear protective screens for reception and customer-facing workstations
- **Hospitality screens** — custom guards for hotel reception desks, bars, and restaurant service areas

### Size Configurations

We offer three standard configurations plus fully custom designs:

1. **Size 1 — Small** — standard configuration for compact counters
2. **Size 2 — Large** — standard configuration for wider service areas
3. **Size 3 — Design Your Own** — fully custom dimensions to your exact measurements

For bespoke sneeze guards, we work from your measurements including base depth, base width, hole dimensions, and overall guard height.

### Material Options

- **Acrylic** — lightweight, optically clear, and cost-effective
- **Perspex** — premium branded cast acrylic with exceptional clarity
- **Polycarbonate (Lexan)** — virtually unbreakable for high-traffic environments
- **Plexiglass** — durable thermoplastic with superior light transmission

### Thickness Options

All guards are available in clear finish:

- 3 mm — lightweight applications
- 4.5 mm — standard commercial use
- 6 mm — heavy-duty environments
- 8 mm — maximum durability
