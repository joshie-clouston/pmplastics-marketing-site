## Gold Coast 2D Laser Engraving Services

P&M Plastics provides laser engraving and etching services to individuals and businesses on the Gold Coast, with Australia-wide shipping available. We engrave graphics, logos, text, and more on a large variety of materials — from metals and plastics to timber and multi-colour laminates.

Established in 1991, we are privately owned and operated in Burleigh Heads.

## Materials

Our 2D laser engraving works across a wide range of materials:

- **Metals** — bronze, aluminium, steel
- **Timber** — solid wood, MDF, veneer
- **Plastics** — acrylic, Perspex, and engineering plastics
- **Laminates** — Gravograph 2–4 colour laminates up to 3 mm

## Design Capabilities

We can engrave company logos, custom designs, and text using over 35,000 types of fonts and a comprehensive library of images. Whether you supply your own design or need help creating one, we are here to assist.

For custom designs, provide a vectored file in any of these formats: EPS, AI, CDR, DXF, DWG, IGS, or PDF.

## Engraving Capacity

- **Wood, MDF, and Aluminium Composite Material:** up to 2000 x 3660 mm
- **Laminates:** Gravograph multi-colour laminates up to 3 mm thickness

## Applications

2D laser engraving is ideal for a wide range of commercial and personal applications, including signage, branding, nameplates, awards, industrial labels, and personalised products. The precision of laser engraving ensures crisp, clean results every time.
