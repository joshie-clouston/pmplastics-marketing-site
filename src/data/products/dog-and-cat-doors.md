## Perspex Dog and Cat Door Panels

P&M Plastics supplies clear [Perspex](/materials/perspex) panels with integrated PetSafe Staywell cat and dog doors. Simply provide your existing glass panel dimensions and your pet's weight, and we supply a matching Perspex panel with the door pre-installed and ready to use.

### How It Works

1. Measure your existing glass panel dimensions
2. Tell us your pet's weight in kilograms
3. We manufacture a Perspex panel to match, with the PetSafe Staywell door pre-installed
4. Replace your old glass panel with the new Perspex panel

The Perspex panel is fully removable, so you can swap back to your original glass at any time — perfect for renters.

### Door Options

#### PetSafe Staywell Slim 4 Way Pet Door — Glass 200
- Suitable for cats and small dogs
- Weight limit: up to 10 kg

#### PetSafe Staywell Original 2 Way Pet Door Medium — 700
- Designed for dogs
- Weight limit: up to 18 kg

### Panel Options

- **Clear Perspex** — virtually invisible in your door frame
- **Tinted Perspex** — available for additional privacy or UV reduction
