## Clear Cast Acrylic Tube

Clear cast acrylic tube offers excellent clarity and high impact strength. Manufactured through a casting process using superior grade acrylic resin, cast tubes are UV stable and suited for both internal and external applications.

### Why Choose Cast Acrylic Tube?

- **Excellent clarity** — superior optical quality from the casting process
- **High impact strength** — durable and robust
- **UV stable** — suitable for outdoor and sun-exposed installations
- **Large diameters available** — up to 1000 mm outer diameter
- **Thicker wall options** — for heavy-duty and structural applications

### Available Sizes

| Outer Diameter | Standard Length |
|---|---|
| 127–350 mm | 1.2 m |
| 400–600 mm | 1.0 m |
| 800–1000 mm | 2.0 m |

**Continuous cast acrylic tube** is also available in 652–685 mm OD in lengths of 1.2–2.0 m.
