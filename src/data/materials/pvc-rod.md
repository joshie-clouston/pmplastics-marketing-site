## What Is PVC Rod?

PVC (Poly Vinyl Chloride) extruded rod offers excellent chemical and mechanical properties with remarkable anti-corrosive characteristics. Available in light grey, PVC rod is ideal for machining into components for chemical processing and industrial applications.

## Key Properties

- **Chemical resistance:** Very high — resists acids, alkaline solutions, alcohols, oils, fats, aliphatic hydrocarbons, and petrol
- **Electrical insulation:** Excellent
- **Thermoformable:** Yes
- **Moisture resistance:** Strong
- **Dimensional stability:** Good
- **Flammability:** Self-extinguishing
- **Specific gravity:** 1.35
- **Bondable and weldable:** Yes

## Chemical Compatibility

**Compatible with:** Acids, alkaline solutions, alcohols, oils, fats, aliphatic hydrocarbons, and petrol.

**Incompatible with:** Benzole, chlorinated hydrocarbons, esters, and ketones.

**Note:** Not recommended for external use. Not FDA-approved for food industry applications.

## Available Sizes

**Colour:** Light Grey only

**2-metre lengths:** 6, 8, 10, 12, 16, 20, 25, 30, 35, 40, 45, 50, 60, 70, 80, 90, 100 mm diameters

**0.5-metre lengths:** 110, 120, 130, 140, 150 mm diameters

## Applications

PVC rod is commonly used for chemical storage vessels, tank liners and fittings, fume cupboards, pump components, and electrical insulators.
