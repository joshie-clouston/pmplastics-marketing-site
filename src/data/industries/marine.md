## Marine Plastic Products

P&M Plastics has been supplying vacuum-formed, UV-resistant plastic glove boxes and driving consoles for marine vessels on the Gold Coast and beyond. We serve OEM clients and manufacture a comprehensive range of marine components.

### What We Make

Our marine product range includes:

- **Driving consoles and glove boxes** — vacuum-formed from UV-resistant plastics for long-lasting durability in harsh marine environments
- **Windscreens** — thermoformed and fabricated from acrylic and polycarbonate
- **PVC air intake manifolds** — precision-manufactured for marine engine applications
- **Fishing rod and tackle supports** — machined from high-density polyethylene (HDPE)
- **Cutting boards, storage pockets, and cup holders** — practical fittings for fishing and pleasure craft
- **Marine ply frames and internal walls** — for cupboards, bed enclosures, and vessel interiors
- **Boat rollers and plastic skids** — engineered for boat trailer and launching applications
- **Stainless steel vents and nameplates** — laser-cut and engraved finishing touches

We supply complete components direct to OEM boat builders, ready for installation — reducing assembly time and ensuring consistent quality across production runs.
