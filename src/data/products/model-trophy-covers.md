## Perspex Model and Trophy Covers

Protect your model car, boat, or trophy from dust — place a clear [Perspex](/materials/perspex) cover over them. Each cover is custom-made to order, ensuring a perfect fit for your prized possessions.

### Specifications

- **Material**: 3mm or 4.5mm clear Perspex
- **Base options**: Perspex base or wooden base included
- **[Engraving](/services/2d-laser-engraving-etching)**: Name plates and inscriptions available

### Custom Sizing

Every cover is made to your exact dimensions. Simply provide:

- Length
- Width
- Height
- Base preference (Perspex or wood)
- Any desired inscriptions or engravings

### Why Perspex?

Clear Perspex offers excellent optical clarity, allowing your display pieces to be admired without compromise. It is lighter and more impact-resistant than glass, making it a safer choice for valuable items.

### Engraving Services

Add a personalised touch with laser-engraved name plates. Ideal for trophies, awards, and special-edition collectables.
