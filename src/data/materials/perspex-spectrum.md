## Bold, Vibrant Colour for Every Application

Perspex Spectrum is a cast acrylic sheet range offering bold, consistent colour performance across 11 popular hues. Designed for signage, architectural features, and interior design, the Spectrum range delivers vivid colour that holds up under both natural and artificial light.

With excellent UV resistance and a 10-year guarantee, Perspex Spectrum performs reliably in both indoor and outdoor environments.

## Available Colours

11 standard colours are available including White/Opal, Green, Yellow, Orange, Red, Pink, and Blue variants — covering the most popular corporate imaging and design hues.

## Specifications

| Property | Value |

| Sheet sizes | 3050 x 2030mm, 1525 x 2030mm, 3050 x 1015mm |

### Opal Thickness Options

- **1TL1** (Dark opacity) — available in 3mm
- **1TL2** (Light opacity) — available in 6mm

## Key Features

- **Bold, consistent colour** that performs in both daylight and artificial light
- **Suitable for fabrication and thermoforming** using standard acrylic processing methods
- **Excellent UV resistance** for long-lasting outdoor colour performance
- **Up to 17 times the impact resistance of glass**
- **100% recyclable**
- **10-year guarantee** covering internal and external use

## Typical Applications

Perspex Spectrum is used for internal and external signage, architectural features, interior design, directional systems, and lighting effects where bold, reliable colour is essential.
