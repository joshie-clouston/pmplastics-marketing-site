## Clear Acrylic Bubble Rod

Clear acrylic bubble rod is used for a wide range of applications — from back-lit staircase supports to hanging chandeliers. The bubble design creates unique light transmission effects that are enhanced dramatically with colour LED lighting.

### Why Choose Bubble Rod?

- **Distinctive visual effect** — the bubble design creates eye-catching light patterns
- **LED compatible** — use with coloured LEDs for stunning effects
- **Versatile** — suits both functional and decorative applications
- **Clear acrylic** — durable and lightweight
