## What Is Rigid PVC Sheet?

Rigid PVC (Poly Vinyl Chloride) extruded sheet offers excellent chemical and mechanical properties with remarkable anti-corrosive characteristics. It is one of the most versatile engineering plastics, used extensively in chemical processing and industrial applications.

## Key Properties

- **Chemical resistance:** Very high — resists acids, alkaline solutions, alcohols, oils, fats, aliphatic hydrocarbons, and petrol
- **Electrical insulation:** Excellent
- **Thermoformable:** Yes
- **Moisture resistance:** Excellent
- **Dimensional stability:** Good
- **Flammability:** Self-extinguishing
- **Specific gravity:** 1.35
- **Bondable and weldable:** Yes
- **UV treatment:** Available for external use with strong weather resistance

## Chemical Compatibility

**Compatible with:** Acids, alkaline solutions, alcohols, oils, fats, aliphatic hydrocarbons, and petrol.

**Incompatible with:** Benzole, chlorinated hydrocarbons, esters, and ketones.

**Note:** Not FDA-approved for food industry applications.

## Available Sizes

**Standard sheet size:** 1220 x 2440 mm (8 x 4 ft)

**Thickness options:**
- White / Light Grey: 3, 4.5, 6, 8, 10, 12, 15, 20, 25 mm
- Clear: 3, 4.5 mm

**Colours:** Clear, White, Light Grey

## Applications

Rigid PVC sheet is commonly used for chemical storage vessels, tank liners and fittings, fume cupboards, pump components, and electrical insulators.
