## Our Materials Range

P&M Plastics stocks one of the most comprehensive ranges of engineering and specialty plastics on the Gold Coast. Whether you need a single cut piece or bulk sheet supply, we have the material and the expertise to match.

## Engineering Plastics

### Transparent and Glazing

- **[Polycarbonate](/materials/polycarbonate)** — 250x stronger than glass, impact-resistant glazing
- **[PETG Sheet](/materials/petg-sheet)** — High clarity with excellent thermoformability
- **[Prismatic Sheet](/materials/prismatic-sheet)** — Light-diffusing panels for skylights and fittings
- **[Skylight Diffusers](/materials/skylight-diffusers)** — Opal and prismatic replacement panels

### PVC Products

- **[PVC Sheet (Rigid)](/materials/pvc-sheet)** — Chemical-resistant sheet for industrial applications
- **[PVC Rod](/materials/pvc-rod)** — Machinable rod for components and fittings
- **[Foam PVC](/materials/foam-pvc)** — Lightweight board for signage and displays

### Polyethylene

- **[HDPE](/materials/hdpe)** — High-density polyethylene sheet and rod
- **[UHMWPE](/materials/uhmwpe-sheet-rod)** — Ultra-high molecular weight for extreme wear applications

### High-Performance Engineering

- **[Acetal (Delrin)](/materials/acetal-sheet-rod)** — Low-friction precision machining plastic
- **[Nylon](/materials/nylon-sheet-rod)** — Self-lubricating gears, bearings and wear components
- **[PTFE (Teflon)](/materials/ptfe)** — Universal chemical resistance, lowest friction
- **[Polypropylene](/materials/polypropylene-sheet-rod)** — Lightweight chemical-resistant sheet and rod

### Signage and Display

- **[HIPS](/materials/hips)** — High impact polystyrene for vacuum forming and displays
- **[Corflute](/materials/corflute)** — Fluted polypropylene for signage
- **[Polypropylene Corflute](/materials/polypropylene-corflute-sheet)** — Heavy-duty fluted sheet
- **[Plaspanel](/materials/plaspanel)** — Polypropylene honeycomb panel
- **[Aluminium Composite Panel](/materials/aluminium-composite-panel)** — Decorative sandwich panel

### Lighting

- **[Eggcrate Louvres](/materials/eggcrate-louvres)** — Grid panels for light fittings and ventilation

## Specialty Products

- **[Self-Adhesive Foam Tape](/materials/self-adhesive-foam-tape)** — Sealing, cushioning and mounting tape
