## Shopfitting & Signage

P&M Plastics manufactures a range of commercial shopfitting components and assemblies for Australia's largest retailers. We supply major signage and shopfitting companies with components and finished products ready for installation across the country.

### What We Make

#### Retail Display Solutions
- Hat, sunglass, and watch display stands
- Acrylic dump bins
- Promotional displays
- Cosmetic display dispensers
- Acrylic slat wall adapters
- Point of sale (POS) displays

#### Signage
- Illuminated signs
- Directional signage
- Custom branded signage for retail and commercial environments

### Clients We Supply

We manufacture shopfitting and signage components for some of Australia's best-known retailers, including:

- K-Mart
- Woolworths
- Coles
- Myer
- David Jones
- Super Cheap Auto
- Caltex service stations
- Various franchise stores

### Materials

Our shopfitting and signage products are manufactured from a range of high-quality materials:

- **High-impact acrylics** — crystal-clear display components
- **Polycarbonate** — tough, shatter-resistant signage
- **MDF board** — structural display elements
- **Aluminium composite materials** — lightweight sign panels
- **Aluminium, steel, and stainless steel** — durable hardware and structural components
