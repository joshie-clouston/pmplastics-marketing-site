## Material Properties Reference

P&M Plastics stocks a comprehensive range of plastic materials. This reference guide provides the key technical properties for each material to help you select the right product for your application.

### Acrylic (Perspex / Plexiglass)

| Property | Value |
|---|---|
| Specific gravity | 1.19 |
| Light transmission (clear) | ~92% |
| Impact strength vs glass | ~17x stronger |
| Deflection temperature under load | 75-100 °C |
| Continuous service temperature | 50 °C |
| Flammability | Combustible |
| Chemical resistance | Resistant to household detergents, cleaning agents, inorganic acids, alkalies, and aliphatic hydrocarbons |
| Incompatible with | Chlorinated and aromatic hydrocarbons, esters, and ketones |

**Available formats:** Sheet (various sizes), cut to size, tubes, rods. Clear and coloured options. Perspex clear carries a 30-year warranty; coloured variants carry a 10-year warranty.

### High Impact Acrylic (H.I.A.)

| Property | Value |
|---|---|
| Specific gravity | 1.15 |
| Light transmission (clear) | ~90% |
| Impact strength | Several times greater than glass |
| Weather resistance | Excellent — withstands sun, cold, and salt spray |
| Standard sheet size | 1270 x 2540 mm |
| Thicknesses | 3 mm, 4.5 mm, 6 mm |
| Chemical resistance | Resistant to ammonia, diluted acids, and aliphatic hydrocarbons |

**Available formats:** Sheet, oversized sheets, and roll stock. Clear and opal finishes.

### Polycarbonate (Lexan)

| Property | Value |
|---|---|
| Impact strength vs glass | ~250x stronger |
| Light transmission (clear) | ~88% |
| Fire resistance | Self-extinguishing |
| Temperature range | -40 °C to 120 °C |
| UV resistance | UV-stabilised grades available |
| Chemical resistance | Resistant to dilute acids, aliphatic hydrocarbons, and many cleaning agents |

**Available formats:** Sheet (various sizes), cut to size. Clear, opal, and tinted options.

### PVC (Polyvinyl Chloride)

| Property | Value |
|---|---|
| Specific gravity | 1.30-1.45 |
| Chemical resistance | Excellent — resistant to acids, alkalis, and most inorganic chemicals |
| Fire resistance | Self-extinguishing |
| Temperature range | Up to 60 °C (rigid) |
| Machinability | Excellent — easily cut, welded, and fabricated |

**Available formats:** Sheet, rod, tube, and fabricated components. Grey and white standard colours.

### HDPE (High Density Polyethylene)

| Property | Value |
|---|---|
| Specific gravity | 0.94-0.97 |
| Chemical resistance | Excellent — resistant to most acids, alkalis, and solvents |
| UV resistance | Good with UV-stabilised grades |
| Temperature range | -50 °C to 80 °C |
| FDA approved | Food-safe grades available |
| Machinability | Excellent — machines cleanly with no moisture absorption |

**Available formats:** Sheet, rod, and machined components. Natural (white) and black standard colours.

### Polypropylene

| Property | Value |
|---|---|
| Specific gravity | 0.90-0.91 |
| Chemical resistance | Excellent — superior to HDPE for many solvents |
| Temperature range | Up to 100 °C |
| Weldability | Excellent — hot air and extrusion welded |

**Available formats:** Sheet, rod, and fabricated tanks and assemblies. Natural and grey standard colours.

### PETG

| Property | Value |
|---|---|
| Light transmission (clear) | ~90% |
| Impact resistance | Superior to acrylic |
| Chemical resistance | Good — resistant to many household and industrial chemicals |
| Food safe | Yes — FDA approved |
| Thermoformability | Excellent |

**Available formats:** Sheet, cut to size. Clear standard.

### Selection Guide

| Application | Recommended Material |
|---|---|
| Signage and displays | Acrylic / Perspex |
| Pool fence panels | Acrylic (standard) or Polycarbonate (high-traffic) |
| Skylights | Acrylic opal or High Impact Acrylic |
| Chemical storage tanks | PVC or Polypropylene |
| Machine guards | Polycarbonate |
| Marine components | UV-stabilised acrylic or HDPE |
| Food processing | HDPE (food-grade) or PETG |
| External signage | High Impact Acrylic |
