## Metallic Shimmer That Shifts with Light

Perspex Pearlescent is a single-sided acrylic sheet that uses light reflection at varying angles to create unique visual effects. The metallic content within the sheet produces a subtle shimmer that changes appearance depending on the light source and viewing direction.

Available in six colours ranging from natural tones to stronger gold and platinum metallics, Perspex Pearlescent adds a premium, sophisticated element to any design.

## Available Colours

Six pearlescent colour options are available, ranging from natural subtle metallics through to stronger gold and platinum finishes.
