## Cool, Soft Pastels with Dual-Finish Versatility

Perspex Sweet Pastels features a wide range of subtle colours that are cooler and softer than traditional acrylic hues — vibrant and inspiring pastel shades designed for modern retail and interior design.

Each sheet features a **matt finish on one side and smooth gloss on the other**, giving designers the flexibility to use both sides for expanded colour and texture applications.

## Available Colours (8 Options)

- **Candy Floss Blue**
- **Candy Floss Pink**
- **Raspberry Sherbet**
- **Lemon Bonbon**
- **Bubblegum Blue**
- **Purple**
- **Spearmint Green**
- **Orange Fizz**

## Key Features

- **Dual-finish surface** — matt on one side, gloss on the other for design versatility
- **EU food contact compliant** — suitable for food display and retail food environments
- **Excellent UV and weather resistance** for long-lasting outdoor colour
- **Suitable for fabrication and [thermoforming](/services/plastic-thermo-forming)** using standard acrylic processing
- **Lightweight and durable** with consistent colour throughout
- **Up to 17 times the impact resistance of glass**
- **100% recyclable**
- **10-year guarantee** covering both external and internal applications

## Typical Applications

Perspex Sweet Pastels is ideal for signage, point-of-sale displays, interior design features, directional systems, and light boxes where soft, modern colour adds visual appeal.
