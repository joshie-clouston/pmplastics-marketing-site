## Acrylic Cut to Size — Custom Perspex Cutting

[Perspex](/materials/perspex), otherwise known as acrylic, is well known for its crystal clarity, impact resistance, and excellent weather ability. Our cut-to-size service delivers acrylic sheet precision-cut to your exact dimensions.

### Why Choose Our Cut-to-Size Service?

- **Precision cutting** — exact dimensions to your specifications
- **Any shape or size** — from small display pieces to large panels
- **Clean edges** — professional finish ready for installation
- **Fast turnaround** — local Gold Coast manufacturing

### Material Properties

Acrylic cut-to-size sheet shares all the properties of standard acrylic:

- Approximately 17 times stronger than glass
- 92% light transmission in clear
- Resistant to household detergents, cleaning agents, and inorganic solutions
- Easy to machine, drill, and glue

### Technical Properties

| Property | Value |
|---|---|
| Specific gravity | 1.19 |
| Light transmission (clear) | ~92% |
| Deflection temperature under load | 75–100 °C |
| Continuous service temperature | 50 °C |
| Flammability | Combustible material |

### Chemical Resistance

Acrylic is unaffected by household detergents, cleaning agents, and inorganic solutions. It is incompatible with chlorinated and aromatic hydrocarbons, esters, and ketones.
