## Gold Coast Laser Cutting Services

P&M Plastics provides CNC laser cutting services using state-of-the-art machinery and laser technologies. Established in 1991, we are privately owned and operated on the Gold Coast, offering precision laser cutting to individuals and businesses with Australia-wide shipping.

## Range of Materials

Our laser cutting capability covers a broad range of materials:

- **Plastics** — [acrylic](/materials/acrylic-sheet), engineering plastics, and specialty materials (up to 30 mm thickness)
- **Timber** — MDF and plywood (up to 25 mm thickness)
- **Metals** — chrome alloy, titanium, Bisalloy, aluminium, and stainless steel

## Cutting Capacity

- **Bed size:** up to 1500 x 3000 mm
- **Steel sign faces:** up to 6.5 metres
- **File formats:** EPS, AI, CDR, DWG, DXF, PDF

## Key Advantages

- **Precision** — laser cutting accuracy is maintained within very close tolerances
- **Consistency** — CNC control delivers identical shapes and cutting times across production runs
- **Speed** — fast turnaround with typical lead times of 3–5 days
- **Efficiency** — CAD software and nesting programs minimise material waste
- **Eco-friendly** — more environmentally friendly than many alternative cutting methods

## Consistent and Custom Designs

Whether you need consistent production runs or fully customised one-off designs, our laser cutting service handles both with equal precision. From intricate jewellery and cake toppers to large-format signage and architectural screens, the accuracy and speed of laser cutting makes it the ideal choice for a wide range of projects.

Provide your design in any standard vector format (EPS, AI, CDR, DWG, DXF, PDF) or send us a JPEG with dimensions for quoting.
