## Decorative Designer Acrylic Panel Gallery

P&M Plastics supplies and fabricates decorative designer acrylic panels in a wide range of colours and finishes. These panels bring visual impact to residential and commercial interiors — offering a lightweight, shatter-resistant, and cost-effective alternative to glass.

### Featured Applications

Our decorative panel portfolio includes:

- **Feature walls and interior cladding** — bold colour panels transforming lobbies, reception areas, and residential living spaces
- **Kitchen and bathroom splashbacks** — hygienic, easy-to-clean panels in vibrant colours as an alternative to tiled or glass splashbacks
- **Retail displays and signage** — eye-catching coloured panels for branded environments and shopfront displays
- **Architectural facades and partitions** — translucent and opaque panels for office dividers, screening, and decorative building elements
- **Furniture and joinery accents** — tabletop inserts, cabinet doors, and custom joinery components in coloured acrylic
- **Illuminated panels** — backlit acrylic panels for bars, restaurants, feature lighting, and commercial interiors

### Available Colours and Finishes

Our decorative panel range includes:

- White, ivory, and grey
- Blue, green, and amber
- Red, orange, yellow, and magenta
- Black and clear
- Perspex Frost (double-sided matt finish)
- Perspex Naturals (earthy tones and textures)
- Perspex Pearlescent (metallic shimmer)
- Perspex Sweet Pastels (soft pastel shades)
- Custom colours available on request

### Key Properties

- Approximately 17 times stronger than glass
- Weather resistant and UV stable
- Easy to cut, shape, and fabricate
- Shatter-resistant — safer than glass
- Light transmission of approximately 92% in clear
- Specific gravity: 1.19

All panels are custom cut to your exact specifications using CNC laser cutting and CNC router cutting, with edge finishing and polishing available.
