## What Is Polypropylene Corflute Sheet?

Polypropylene corflute sheet is a heavy-duty twin-wall extruded polypropylene sheet designed for industrial and construction applications. It offers superior strength and durability compared to standard corflute, making it ideal for surface protection, reusable packaging, and construction hoarding.

## Key Properties

- **Construction:** Twin-wall fluted polypropylene
- **Weight:** Lightweight yet structurally strong
- **Moisture resistance:** Fully water and moisture resistant
- **Chemical resistance:** Resists most chemicals, oils, and solvents
- **Reusability:** Designed for repeated use
- **Recyclability:** Fully recyclable polypropylene
- **UV stabilisation:** UV-stabilised grades available for outdoor use
- **Impact resistance:** High

## Available Sizes

**Standard sheet size:** 2440 x 1220 mm

**Thickness options:** 3 mm (500 GSM), 5 mm (900 GSM), and heavier grades on request

**Colours:** White, Black — other colours available on request.

## Applications

Polypropylene corflute sheet is widely used for construction hoarding, floor and surface protection during building works, reusable packaging and transit cases, agricultural applications, [signage](/industries/shopfitting-signage), and temporary partitions.
