## Custom Liquid Transport Tanks for Emergency Vehicles

Built tough for mobile water delivery — from 200L to 4,000L. P&M Plastics designs and manufactures high-performance water transport tanks tailored for emergency vehicles and mobile applications.

Our tanks are built from black HDPE (High-Density Polyethylene) and engineered to withstand tough Australian conditions. We supply tanks for fire trucks, emergency response vehicles, and utility fleets.

### Key Features

- **Custom capacities**: Sizes from 200L to 4,000L
- **Durable HDPE construction**: Lightweight, corrosion-resistant, and UV-stable
- **Fully customisable**: Inlet and outlet sizes tailored to your requirements
- **Internal baffles**: Baffle systems prevent liquid surge during transport
- **Quality assurance**: Every tank is leak and pressure tested before dispatch
- **Australian standards**: In-house fabrication by certified specialists
- **No rust**: Long-life design that will not corrode

- Firefighting vehicles
- Emergency water supply units
- Road maintenance vehicles

### Tank Building Process

1. **Consultation** — discuss dimensions, capacity, and specific requirements
2. **CAD design** — detailed design created for your approval
3. **Precision fabrication** — manufactured using expert plastic welding techniques
4. **Testing** — full pressure and leak testing on every tank
5. **Delivery** — dispatched installation-ready to your site
