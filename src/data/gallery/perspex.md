## Perspex Fabrication Gallery

P&M Plastics is an authorised Queensland reseller of the full Perspex range and has decades of experience fabricating this world-leading cast [acrylic](/materials/acrylic-sheet) into precision components and finished products.

### What You'll See Here

Our Perspex fabrication portfolio showcases the breadth of work we deliver across residential, commercial, and industrial sectors:

- **Custom signage and branding** — [laser-cut](/services/cnc-laser-cutting) Perspex logos, illuminated sign panels, and corporate imaging in colour-matched Perspex for national retailers
- **Architectural installations** — room dividers, partitions, feature walls, and balustrade infill panels fabricated from clear and coloured Perspex sheet
- **Display products** — point-of-sale stands, product dispensers, and retail display cases in crystal-clear Perspex
- **Pool fence panels** — custom-cut Perspex panels compliant with Gold Coast City Council and QBCC pool fencing regulations
- **[Skylight covers](/products/skylight-cover-replacements)** — opal and clear Perspex replacement panels cut to size for residential and commercial buildings
- **Furniture and joinery accents** — tabletops, shelving, and decorative elements in Perspex Frost, Naturals, and Pearlescent finishes

### Materials Featured

Projects in this gallery use materials from the complete Perspex collection, including Perspex Clear, Perspex Fluorescent, Perspex Spectrum LED, Perspex Frost, Perspex Naturals, Perspex Pearlescent, and Perspex Sweet Pastels. All carry a 30-year warranty (clear) or 10-year warranty (coloured).

### Fabrication Techniques

Our Perspex projects are manufactured using CNC laser cutting, [CNC router cutting](/services/cnc-router-cutting), [thermoforming](/services/plastic-thermo-forming), polishing, and precision gluing — all performed in-house at our Burleigh Heads workshop.
