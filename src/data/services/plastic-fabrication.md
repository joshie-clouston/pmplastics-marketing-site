## Gold Coast Plastic Fabrication Services

Plastic fabrication covers the most diversified range of processes performed on thermoplastic materials. At P&M Plastics, our services include cutting, machining, line bending, welding, gluing, diamond polishing, drilling, and assembly across a wide variety of thermoplastic materials.

With over 30 years of industry experience, we handle jobs of every scale — from water tanks and large-format signage through to small retail fittings and one-off prototypes.

## Our CNC Capabilities

We operate modern CNC equipment to deliver precision results across every project:

- **CNC Water Jet Cutting** — cuts all types of plastics and other materials into virtually any shape, text, or design
- **CNC Vacuum Forming** — multiple specialised machines with a variety of applications
- **CNC Router Cutting** — versatile machines for cutting a wide selection of plastics and other materials
- **CNC Laser Cutting** — competitive pricing with superb turnaround for various materials
- **Plastic Thermoforming** — specialised ovens for multiple forming applications
- **2D and 3D Laser Engraving** — detailed engraving and etching with design assistance available

## Materials We Work With

We maintain a large inventory of thermoplastic materials, including:

- Perspex and acrylic (sheets, rods, and tubes)
- Polycarbonate
- High Impact Polystyrene (HIPS)
- PETG
- PVC
- HDPE
- Acetal
- PTFE
- Polypropylene
- UHMWPE
- Nylon
- Specialty materials

## Notable Projects

Our team has delivered projects ranging from large Kmart opal acrylic signage fabrication, to hot air welding of HDPE and PVC polypropylene water tanks for motorhomes, to line bending of clear acrylic sneeze guards for the retail food industry.
