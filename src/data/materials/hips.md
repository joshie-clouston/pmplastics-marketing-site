## What Is HIPS?

High Impact Polystyrene (HIPS) is an extensively used material in vacuum forming applications. It combines high impact resistance with excellent thermoformability, making it ideal for point-of-sale displays, packaging, and refrigerator linings.

## Key Properties

- **Impact strength:** Excellent inherent toughness
- **Thermoformability:** Superior — no pre-drying required
- **Odour and taste:** Odourless and tasteless
- **Dimensional stability:** Good
- **Water absorption:** Low
- **Chemical resistance:** Solid
- **Printability:** Excellent decorability and printability
- **Food safety:** FDA approved

**Note:** Not recommended for external use due to limited UV resistance.

## Available Sizes

**Sheet thicknesses:** 0.50 mm to 3.0 mm

**Standard dimensions:** 1370 x 760 mm or 2440 x 1220 mm — custom sizes available on request.

**Colours:** Black and White in stock; other colours by special order.

**Finishes:** Haircell and smooth options available.

**Note:** Sold in full sheets only.

## Applications

HIPS is widely used for point-of-sale displays, food packaging trays, signage, housewares and toys, bathroom cabinets, refrigerator crispers, and screen printing applications.
