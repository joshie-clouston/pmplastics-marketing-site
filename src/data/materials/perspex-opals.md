## Even Light Diffusion in Every Shade of White

Perspex Opals is a white acrylic sheet offering a wide range of different light transmissions, from dense white to highly translucent whites. The material refracts and diffuses light to create smooth, even illumination for stunning visual effects — making it the go-to choice for light boxes and illuminated signage.

## Light Transmission Options

| Code | Light Transmission | Available Thicknesses |
|------|-------------------|----------------------|
| 030 | 70% (highly translucent) | 3mm, 6mm |
| 040 | 51% | 3mm |
| 050 | 37% | 3mm |
| 069 | 9% (dense white) | 3mm, 6mm |

## Sheet Sizes

- 3050 x 2030mm
- 1525 x 2030mm
- 3050 x 1015mm

## Key Features

- **Multiple light transmission levels** — choose the right opacity for your application
- **Excellent light diffusion** — creates smooth, even illumination without hot spots
- **UV and weather resistant** for long-lasting indoor and outdoor performance
- **Suitable for fabrication and thermoforming** using standard acrylic methods
- **Lightweight** with consistent colour throughout
- **Up to 17 times the impact resistance of glass**
- **10-year guarantee** covering both external and internal applications

## Typical Applications

Perspex Opals is ideal for signage (internal and external), point-of-sale displays, light boxes, illuminated displays, and architectural lighting features where even, diffused light is essential.
