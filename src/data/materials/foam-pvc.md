## What Is Foam PVC?

Foamed Poly Vinyl Chloride (Foam PVC) is a semi-rigid extruded sheet material available in multiple colours. At half the weight of rigid PVC, it delivers excellent versatility for sign, display, exhibition, and building industry applications.

## Key Properties

- **Weight:** Half the weight of rigid PVC
- **Chemical resistance:** Very high — resists acids, alkaline solutions, alcohols, oils, fats, hydrocarbons, and petrol
- **Moisture resistance:** Very good
- **Dimensional stability:** Good
- **Water absorption:** Low
- **Flammability:** Self-extinguishing
- **Specific gravity:** 0.75
- **Weathering resistance:** Excellent
- **Thermoformable:** Yes

## Chemical Compatibility

**Compatible with:** Acids, alkaline solutions, alcohols, oils, fats, hydrocarbons, and petrol.

**Incompatible with:** Benzole, chlorinated hydrocarbons, esters, and ketones.

## Available Sizes

**Standard sheet size:** 1220 x 2440 mm (8 x 4 ft)

**3 mm thickness colours:** White, Blue, Black, Yellow, Green, Grey, Red

**Other thicknesses (White only):** 1, 2, 4.5, 6, 8, 10 mm

## Applications

Foam PVC is widely used for screen printing, vinyl graphics, photo print mounting, cooling insulation, partition boards, industrial linings, display and exhibition builds, and general building materials.
