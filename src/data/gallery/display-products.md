## Display Products Gallery

P&M Plastics manufactures a comprehensive range of display products and cases for retail, commercial, and museum environments. Our display products are supplied to some of Australia's largest retailers and [shopfitting](/industries/shopfitting-signage) companies.

### Featured Products

Our display portfolio includes:

- **Point-of-sale (POS) displays** — branded product displays, countertop stands, and promotional units for retail environments
- **Hat, sunglass, and watch display stands** — custom-designed stands in clear and coloured [acrylic](/materials/acrylic-sheet) for fashion and accessories retailers
- **Acrylic dump bins** — durable promotional bins for high-traffic retail areas
- **Cosmetic display dispensers** — precision-fabricated dispensers and testers for beauty counters
- **Acrylic slat wall adapters** — modular display components for shopfitting systems
- **Museum and gallery display cases** — UV-filtering [Perspex](/materials/perspex) VA and VE gallery-grade cases for artefact and artwork protection
- **Trophy and memorabilia cases** — custom-sized enclosures for sporting clubs, schools, and corporate offices
- **Menu and brochure holders** — countertop and wall-mounted holders in clear acrylic

### Materials

- **Perspex** — premium cast acrylic with exceptional clarity and a 30-year warranty on clear
- **High Impact Acrylic** — superior impact resistance for high-traffic environments
- **[Polycarbonate](/materials/polycarbonate) (Lexan)** — virtually unbreakable for security-sensitive displays
- **Perspex VA/VE Gallery Grade** — museum-grade UV protection for preserving valuable items

### Fabrication Techniques

Display products are manufactured using a combination of [CNC laser cutting](/services/cnc-laser-cutting), [CNC router cutting](/services/cnc-router-cutting), precision bending, flame polishing, diamond polishing, and solvent welding. All products are assembled and quality-checked in-house before dispatch.

### Clients

We supply display products to major Australian retailers including K-Mart, Woolworths, Coles, Myer, David Jones, Super Cheap Auto, and Caltex service stations.
