## Clear Acrylic Tubes — Extruded and Cast

P&M Plastics stocks an extensive range of extruded and cast clear acrylic tubes suitable for displays, lighting, fabrication, and custom manufacturing.

### Extruded Clear Acrylic Tube

Extruded clear acrylic tube is manufactured using the extrusion process and offers very good clarity. It can be machined and glued for use in all types of displays and products.

- **Best for** — indoor applications, displays, fabricated assemblies
- **Not recommended** for external or UV-exposed applications

### Cast Clear Acrylic Tube

Cast clear acrylic tube is manufactured in a mould using a superior grade of acrylic resin. It offers excellent clarity and is UV stable.

- **Best for** — external applications, projects requiring superior optical clarity
- **UV stable** — suitable for outdoor and sun-exposed installations
