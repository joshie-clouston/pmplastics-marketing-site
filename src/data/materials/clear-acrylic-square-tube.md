## Clear Acrylic Square Tube

Clear acrylic square tube offers excellent optical clarity in a clean, square profile — ideal for displays, structural supports, and custom fabrication. Available in both extruded and cast grades to suit indoor and outdoor applications.

### Why Choose Square Tube?

- **Clean lines** — the square profile suits modern, architectural designs
- **Excellent clarity** — transparent acrylic with superior light transmission
- **Machinable** — easy to cut, drill, and glue
- **Versatile** — suits displays, structures, and decorative features

### Extruded vs Cast

- **Extruded** — very good clarity, suited for indoor applications, machinable and glueable
- **Cast** — excellent clarity, UV stable, suited for external applications
