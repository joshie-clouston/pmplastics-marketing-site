## High Impact Acrylic Sheet (H.I.A.)

High Impact Acrylic is an extruded [acrylic](/materials/acrylic-sheet) product offering superior impact resistance — several times greater than glass — combined with excellent optical clarity and weather resistance. Engineered for demanding external applications, H.I.A. withstands exposure to blazing sun, extreme cold, sudden temperature changes, and salt water spray.

### Why Choose High Impact Acrylic?

- **Superior impact resistance** — significantly outperforms standard acrylic and glass
- **Excellent optical clarity** — approximately 90% light transmission in clear
- **Outstanding weather resistance** — proven in harsh Australian conditions
- **Lightweight** — specific gravity of just 1.15
- **Versatile fabrication** — cut to size with corner radiusing and edge finishing

- Scrape finish
- Flame polish
- Full polish

### Chemical Resistance

H.I.A. is resistant to ammonia, diluted acids, and aliphatic hydrocarbons. It is incompatible with chlorinated and aromatic hydrocarbons, esters, and ketones.

### Flammability

High impact acrylic is a combustible material and must be considered for building material applications.
