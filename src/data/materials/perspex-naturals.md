## Inspired by Nature

Perspex Naturals is a cast acrylic collection inspired by the aesthetics of the natural world. The range features a double-sided matt surface that simulates the earthy tones and textures of natural stone — delivering the look of sandblasted or etched glass in a lightweight, durable acrylic sheet.

Designed for retail and interior applications, Perspex Naturals brings organic warmth to modern spaces.

## Available Colours (6 Options)

- **Ash Grey**
- **Moonlight White**
- **Walnut Brown**
- **Desert Beige**
- **Mineral Grey**
- **Midnight Black**

- 3050 x 2030mm

## Key Features

- **Double-sided matt finish** for a consistent natural stone appearance
- **Texture retained after [thermoforming](/services/plastic-thermo-forming)** — fabricate with confidence
- **Strong and hard-wearing** — built for demanding environments
- **Excellent UV resistance** suitable for outdoor applications
- **Up to 17 times the impact resistance of glass**
- **100% recyclable**
- **10-year guarantee** covering internal and external use

## Typical Applications

Perspex Naturals is used for signage, architectural features, interior design, partitions, lighting effects, furniture, and artwork where a warm, organic aesthetic is desired.
